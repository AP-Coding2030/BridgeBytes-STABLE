Connecting Healthy Minds in a Digital World

BridgeBytes is a student-led initiative that promotes digital literacy and mental well-being by connecting generations through accessible, engaging technology education.

Learn More

What is BridgeBytes?

BridgeBytes addresses the digital divide by teaching essential technology skills to young children and seniors. Our program blends digital literacy with mental health awareness, empowering participants to feel confident, safe, and connected in an increasingly digital world.

Who We Serve

Young Children and Schools

We introduce foundational digital skills, online safety, and healthy screen habits through interactive, age-appropriate lessons and presentations.

Seniors

We help seniors navigate devices, recognize online scams, and stay connected with loved ones and their community.

What We Do

  • Digital literacy workshops
  • “Spot the Scam” and other interactive activities
  • Digital Literacy and Online Safety Presentations
  • Technology Support
